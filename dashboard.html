Welcome to Dementia Risk Prediction-CogniCare Application

How to Use This Application

1. Caregiver Section

The Caregiver section allows users responsible for individuals with dementia to manage their care routines, receive helpful recommendations, and track the patient's progress. To navigate, click on the "Caregiver" button in the menu above.

2. Patient Section

The Patient section is designed for individuals who want to monitor their cognitive health or those at risk of dementia. Click the "Patient" button to access tools such as dementia risk prediction, cognitive tests, and personalized recommendations.

3. Health Specialist Section

The Health Specialist section enables professionals to assist patients by viewing prediction results and offering expert guidance. Click the "Health Specialist" button to explore the features tailored for professionals.

4. Settings

In the Settings section, users can manage their account details, privacy settings, and notifications. Click the "Settings" button to customize your experience in the application.

5. Getting Started

To start using the application, navigate to your respective section (Caregiver, Patient, or Health Specialist), and follow the instructions provided. You can generate dementia risk predictions, view recommendations, or adjust account settings as needed.

6. Predictions and Recommendations

After navigating to the "Patient" section, use the dementia risk prediction tool by entering the necessary data, and view the results and personalized recommendations. Caregivers and health specialists can use this data to provide better care and guidance.
