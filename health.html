How to Use the Application

1. Predict Dementia (Form A)

This form allows you to predict the risk of dementia based on health metrics.

Steps:

  • Click on "Predict Dementia Form A" in the navigation menu.
  • Complete all required fields with accurate information.
  • Press the "Submit" button to view your prediction result.

2. Predict Dementia (Form B)

Form B provides an alternative method to assess dementia risk.

Steps:

  • Navigate to "Predict Dementia Form B" from the menu.
  • Fill out the form with the specified metrics.
  • Submit to receive your prediction.

3. Personalized Recommendations

Receive tailored advice based on your dementia predictions.

Steps:

  • Click on "Personalized Recommendations" in the navigation menu.
  • Review the recommendations that best suit your needs.

4. Chatbot

Use the chatbot for quick answers to your questions.

Steps:

  • Select "Chatbot" from the navigation menu.
  • Type your query in the chat interface.
  • Follow the responses for guidance on using the application.

5. Settings

Manage your account and notification settings here.

Steps:

  • Click on "Settings" from the menu.
  • Update your personal information and preferences.
  • Save your changes to keep your account secure.
