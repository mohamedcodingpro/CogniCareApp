How to Use the Application

1. Predict Dementia (Form A)

This form allows you to predict the risk of dementia based on various health metrics.

Steps:

  • Click on "Predict Dementia Form A" from the navigation menu.
  • Fill in all the required health and personal details in the form.
  • Click on the "Submit" button to get your dementia risk prediction.

2. Personalized Recommendations

Based on your dementia prediction, you will receive tailored recommendations to support brain health.

Steps:

  • Click on "Personalized Recommendations" in the menu.
  • View recommendations that align with your prediction results, including diet, activities, and lifestyle changes.

3. Community

Engage with the community to share insights and get support from other users.

Steps:

  • Select "Community" from the navigation menu.
  • Browse community posts or share your own experiences and questions related to dementia and health.
  • You can also interact with posts by liking or commenting.

4. Play Memory Match Game

Test and train your memory with a fun and interactive game designed to enhance cognitive skills.

Steps:

  • Click on "Play Memory Match Game" from the menu.
  • Follow the on-screen instructions to start the game and match similar cards.
  • Use this game as a tool to boost your memory and have fun while doing it.

5. Chatbot

Our chatbot helps answer your questions or guides you through different sections of the application.

Steps:

  • Select "Chatbot" from the menu.
  • Type your query or question, and the bot will provide you with helpful information or instructions.

6. Settings

Manage your account settings such as email, password, and notifications.

Steps:

  • Click on "Settings" from the navigation menu.
  • Update your personal information, change your password, or customize your notification preferences.
