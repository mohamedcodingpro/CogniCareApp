Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: What is the purpose of this application?

A: The application aims to help caregivers, patients, and health specialists in monitoring cognitive health and providing appropriate care for individuals at risk of dementia.

Q2: How do I access my account?

A: You can access your account by clicking on the "Settings" button in the header and logging in with your credentials.

Q3: Is my data safe?

A: Yes, we prioritize user privacy and adhere to South African data privacy regulations to ensure your data is secure.

Q4: Can I receive updates and recommendations?

A: Yes, users can opt-in to receive notifications for updates, recommendations, and important information related to dementia care.

Q5: What should I do if I encounter a problem?

A: If you encounter any issues, please reach out to our support team via the "Contact Us" section, and we will assist you promptly.
